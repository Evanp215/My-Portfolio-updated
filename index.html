About Me

I first discovered programming while I was in high school. I had no idea what degree or career path I would pursue, but after a little research and reading on the subject, I was certain this was what I wanted to do. I had a passion for gaming and problem solving, so my first reaction was to attempt programing games through C#. However, after a short time, I became highly interested in designing websites.

I attempted to learn on my own through online resources, videos, and books, but I wasn't making the progress I wanted to see. That's when I found Thinkful online, and after reading about their program, I decided to enroll.

Almost immediately I was welcomed by a large community of other Thinkful students. Shortly after as I began the program, I felt myself learning faster and absorbing more information than I ever could before on my own.
